Treatment Programs

To guarantee continuity of care, we offer three levels of treatment: Partial hospitalization program , Intensive Outpatient Program , and Outpatient services

Our PHP (Partial Hospitalization Program) is our highest level of care. The program typically meets 6 hours a day. 6-7 day a week, and lasts 12 weeks. This program is ideal for people transitioning from a residential inpatient facility whi need more support and, intensive therapy

Our IOP (Intensive Outpatient Program program meets 3-6 hours a day up to 5 days a week, depending on individual needs. To successfully graduate, clients must attend for 12 weeks. This program who need structure yet continue working and attending school part-time

Our lowest intensity level is our OP or Outpatient Program . This program meets 3 hours a day for three days a week. This level of care is ideal for people who need little support as they transition back into fully independent living.
